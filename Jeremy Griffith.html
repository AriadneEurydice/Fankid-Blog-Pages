Synopsis

The son of Chili Griffith and Georgia Herrscher, and the oldest child for both. This Dark type trainer originally wanted to become a member of Unova's Elite Four, but due to being the oldest in his family, he instead stayed home to help Chili. Outside of work, Jeremy likes to hang out with his friends and spends most of his time at the skatepark.

Personality

As the oldest child, Jeremy is very serious and often overprotective of his younger sisters. Unfortunately, Jeremy doesn't always extend this towards other girls in his life. Despite his rough exterior, Jeremy is still a good kid at heart, a hurt kid, but good. Growing up with a lot of instability in his life despite Chili's best efforts forced Jeremy to step up and shoulder responsibilities at a young age.

Backstory

It was an unseasonably hot summer in Iccirus City the year Jeremy was born, even the hospital's industrial air conditioners were fighting to give relief to the residents. It had been in the high ninties for three days by the time Jeremy was born.

From as far back as Jeremy can remember, it has always been him and his dad. Chili always reminds him that it's not completely true, that for a long time they lived with his grandparents, Seneca and Fletcher. And he does have pictures of him as a toddler with them- though he doesn't really remember them. Back then, Chili and Jeremy used to share single room. He doesn't exactly remember what it was like, with just him and his dad because by the time Jeremy was lucid, Isabella was already apart of his family unit.

    From a young age Jeremy had terrible fits that were uncontrollable, often sudden and unprovoked and lasted for at the most, half an hour before they would stop due to fatigue. It often happens when Jeremy is in high stress environments such as school, or situations where he gets easily overwhelmed, and often times he can feel himself starting an episode.

    It is suspected by a.. previous child psychologist that this behavior aligns with Jeremy's home life with the lack of security of food and shelter- even possible emotional abandonment. Suggestions of.. relocation.. were made. (They stopped going after being told that.) Although research from a local library via outdated psychology textbooks has suggested that it is something that one grows out of, unfortunately for Jeremy (and those around him) he is an exception, though he does have a self-claimed “better grip.” He's prone to sudden outbursts of violence and uses sports as a.. creative outlet for his anger.

    Due to having such intense mood swings, Jeremy often practices- fails, but practices in preparing himself for how he's going to react and tries to appear emotionless. But in reality, he is a pretty emotional guy. Due to being unable to regulate his own emotions it leaves him in a perpetual state of fight-or-flight which in turns, makes it worse. He's suspected of having borderline personality disorder but Jer can neither care or afford the diagnostic testing and medications.

    Jeremy's notable relationships.

    Chili Griffith

    Jeremy's father. While Chili and Jeremy often don't see eye to eye when it comes to Jeremy's future (and wasted potential in school), they love each over very deeply and do their best to work together in order to make ends meet for the family. Growing up, Chili did his best to model responsibility, family, and duty to Jeremy. But watching his father struggle from day to day, Jeremy began to see that his father wasn't as perfect as he thought he was. This led Jeremy to secretly hold resentment for his uncles, who "never" made an effort to help Chili. Despite everything, Jeremy is very protective when it comes to his father.

    Georgia Herrscher

    Jeremy's mother. She may have given birth to him but she is not his mother.

    Annabelle Griffith

    Jeremy's younger sister. Despite Anna's late arrival into his life, Jeremy felt an immediate sense of responsibility for her. The two butt heads from time to time but quickly forgive each other and will always take each other's side when in an argument with an outsider. Anna sometimes feels the need to give him unwarranted advice in the same way that she never listens to advice he gives her.

    Isabella Griffith

    Jeremy's younger sister. Jeremy loves Isabella deeply, for as long as he can remember she has always been around for him. She's his youngest sister and not to be that guy, but she's his favorite sister. Jeremy has fond memories of his childhood with Isabella. Two of the worst days of his entire life that come to mind is when he saved her from drowning and when she ran away to start her journey. Jeremy nearly followed her to Kalos but didn't after Anna convinced him that she would be okay on her own. He still worries about her and does his best to keep in contact with her and send her money when he can.

    Tatia Weiss

    Jeremy's girlfriend. From their first meeting Jeremy was enamored with Tatia, and she was his first official girlfriend. Now, Jeremy will admit, he wasn't the perfect boyfriend- but not to point fingers, Tatia wasn't exactly a perfect girlfriend either. He loved her so much it was driving him crazy. They were volatile, destined even. And on and off so often that Jeremy would forget if they were dating or if they were on a break. Doesn't matter- they couldn't keep away from each other anyways. Now, regrettably, it's over and he still loves her.

    Sterling Fontaine

    Jeremy's love interest. They'd both ended relationships for the summer season and found each other, but it wasn't serious enough for them to want to stay together... right?

    Spinner D'Amore

    Jeremy's friend. Even from their first meeting, Jeremy could tell that Spinner was a good guy.. just, not a good guy. Jeremy likes how respectful and responsible he is. He liked the way Spinner treated Anna, how he cared for her. He also admired just how cool he was, how he didn't take himself too seriously. He couldn't even be mad about Spinner's line of work, at least he had a job. A job is job, Jeremy respected the hustle.

Not that there was anything wrong with his own, Paul was just different. He's someone that Jeremy really respects. Even after Tatia moved on from him, her family has never turned him away when he needed them.

    Timeline

    Here is a semi-detailed timeline of Jeremy's life!

    July 19th, 2018 - Jeremy is born.
    On the 19th day, a baby boy is born to Georgia Clarkson and Chili Griffith at Iccirus Medical Center @ 9:59 P.M. in Iccirus City, Unova. Chili is not present for his birth nor did he have any idea that Georgia was pregnant. While excited to meet him, Georgia quickly realizes that she is in over her head but chalks it up to nerves. She struggles to have a connection with her new baby.
    August 7th, 2018 - T-168.
    It's an unfortunate tuesday morning for Chili, he has missed his alarm, spilled egg yolk on his new shirt and it is his first day at new job. The rocky start to his day has given him anxiety, despite the setback he powers though and manages to make it to work with five minutes to spare. Halfway though his day, Chili recieves a distressed call from his sister in law Aria, telling him to come home. Not wanting to make a bad impression, Chili stays the entire day- shutting off his phone. By the time he is on the bus home, he's missed at least 20 calls from Aria and his brother Cilan. Once he's home he's met with Georgia.. and a baby. Georgia quickly explains that he's the father of her baby.. and tells him that she wants nothing to do with either of them. Five minutes later Georgia leaves and Chili is left with an unnamed baby boy and only a week's worth of formula.
    November 24th, 2018 - Sign your life away.
    The adjustment period of being a new parent hit Chili like a train and lasted longer than expected. But it wasn't terrible. While Chili loved his baby almost instantly, he hadn't been able to find a name that suited the baby yet. Despite Cilan warning Chili that he wouldn't be able to help with the baby, Aria was willing to bend the rules a bit and help him. After a few weeks of caring for Jeremy with the help of Aria, Chili finally came to the conclusion that he would keep his son. The birth certificate stamped with ink imprints of the boy's feet had been sitting on his desk for months. He'd been trying out names for some time, changing every few days or so before finally he realized that the baby responded to one name. Jeremy. Chili filled in the baby's name box and signed the certificate so he could take it to be finalized..

    With what seemed to be almost no warning- or maybe Chili had just been so busy it had slipped his mind, Cilan and Aria began the process to move to Lumiose City, Kalos. Up until then, Aria had been Chili's trusted babysitter as she hadn't minded- or he thought she hadn't minded taking care of Jeremy while Chili was at work. He'd been paying her, just what he could afford to pay her and she was a lot of help, but Cilan had been firm on not helping Chili with Jeremy's necessities.

    By April, Cilan and Aria had completed their move, leaving Chili and Jeremy in the home they had all shared. After moving twice, Chili finally cut his losses and packed the family for New Tork City so they could move in with his parents in order to be more financially stable and because his parents, Seneca and Fletcher had been watching over Jeremy. It was just easier to live with them, and allowed him and Jer to sleep in due to grandma and grandpa being across the hall rather than across the city.

    December 18th, 2020 - Isabella is brought home.
    The winter season meant the Griffith household was full of family now, especially with Cilan and Aria bringing along the newest addition of the family- Claudia. When Chili had gotten the call the night before, he'd been nervous to tell anyone and instead decided to leave the house early in order to pick up Isabella. After coming home with her it was well, a shock to the entire family, especially since this time she wasn't even Chili's. Aria in particular.. got.. upset that attention Claudia was recieving was stolen by a new baby girl. Jeremy already loved her, stopping his games every once and while to go and stare at his baby sister.
    Fletcher gets diagnosed with cancer..
    It became harder to deal with Jeremy's grandparents as they grew older, they had become forgetful and were a sobering reality for Chili as he realized he could no longer leave them alone with the kids for long periods of time. When his father got sick with cancer, it put an even bigger strain on his limited resources. Thankfully his mother was still lucid enough to accompany him to his appointments but this forced Chili to send Jeremy and Isabella to daycare after school. Despite working, the bills began to pile up.
    SUMMER, 2025 - Lumi-no!
    With the end of the school year in May marks the beginning of summer break. Jeremy and Bella's vacation started at New Tork's airport and ended in Lumiose City. When Cilan and Aria offered to take the kids for the summer, Chili jumped at the opportunity to take care of his family and catch up on bills- especially daycare services, that had begun to pile up. The months leading up to the summer had hit him particulary hard making ends meet and slowly they had begun to rely more and more on the local food bank in downtown New Tork. After only a week in Lumiose, a an unexpected fight has Chili bring home Jeremy and Isabella early.
    2025 - Fletcher dies.
    The insurance payout is only so much, and barely gets them far enough to last them two months. Suddenly and without warning, Seneca contracts the flu and dies. Chili loses his job out of grief, and thus begins the battle of making ends meet.
    June 12th, 2029 - Family Introduction.
    Annabelle is introduced to the family.
    December 7th, 2029 - The Drowning.
    Seconds. "That's all it takes." It's something Jeremy had heard over and over growing up, that it only takes seconds. The morning of Anna's adoption hearing Chili had woken him early so Jeremy could lock the door behind him. Jeremy stayed up for a bit after they left to get some chores done around the apartment before going back to bed. A few hours later, Jeremy finally dragged himself out of bed to make himself and Bella breakfast, after calling for her three seperate times he went to check her room. Not finding her, Jeremy hurried outside to see if he could find her, thankfully she and Simisear were skating on the pool which had frozen.

    After calling her in for breakfast, Jeremy headed back in to finish serving the food. After waiting another minute or two, Jeremy pulled on a jacket and headed back out outside- only this time Bella wasn't anywhere to be seen. Simisear was running around the pool anxiously... and the ice was cracked. Immediately Jeremy began running down the stairs, and across the court before jumping the fence. Without hesitation, Jeremy jumped into the pool. The cold shocked him enough that he released all his air and he hurried back up for a second before diving back down to pull her out. After about five minutes of cpr, Bella finally threw up what seemed like half of the pool.

    While breathing, Bella was still blue and barely conscious so Jeremy picked her up and hurried to the nearest hospital. Once they arrived Jeremy was given dry clothes, socks, warm blankets and hot chocolate as he waited for the doctors to finish working on her. The nurses all called him a hero while reassuring him that it wasn't anybody's fault that this happened.
